principle_id: INTERVENTION_LADDER_COVERAGE
title: Weak / Medium / Strong Intervention Coverage
scope_stages: solution_factory

Description:
The generated solution space must cover multiple intervention strengths. At least one weak, one medium, and one strong alternative should be explored unless the problem structure makes a class clearly impossible.

Checklist:
- portfolio includes weak, medium, and strong intervention classes
- weak alternatives change flow parameters, not only wording or reporting
- strong alternatives materially change architecture, business model, or role topology
