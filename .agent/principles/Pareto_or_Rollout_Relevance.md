principle_id: PARETO_OR_ROLLOUT_RELEVANCE
title: Pareto-Relevant or Rollout-Relevant Alternatives Only
scope_stages: solution_factory

Description:
An alternative should be kept in the portfolio only if it either adds a new tradeoff position to the local Pareto frontier or acts as a rollout-relevant stepping stone with lower blast radius or higher reversibility.

Checklist:
- each alternative states why it is pareto-relevant or rollout-relevant
- dominated alternatives without rollout value are removed
- weak alternatives are not kept only for decorative diversity
