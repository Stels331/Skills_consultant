principle_id: UNCERTAINTY_ROUTING
title: Route Uncertainty Before Decision
scope_stages: characterization,problem_factory,solution_factory,reporting

Description:
When critical inputs are missing, the system must route the case into clarification, data collection, or bounded pilot design before making a hard management choice.

Checklist:
- missing critical inputs are named explicitly
- next action includes clarification or data collection when needed
- status quo is not auto-promoted as a fake safe decision under uncertainty
