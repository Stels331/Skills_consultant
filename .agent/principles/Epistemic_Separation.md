principle_id: EPISTEMIC_SEPARATION
title: Fact / Inference / Hypothesis Separation
scope_stages: problem_factory,solution_factory,reporting

Description:
Assertions must be separated by epistemic level. Facts, interpretations, hypotheses, and deferred questions may not be merged into one equally certain narrative.

Checklist:
- factual observations are distinguishable from interpretations
- hypotheses are marked as hypotheses or pending checks
- missing inputs do not silently transform into hard conclusions
