# Layer 4: Allocation Model

This layer maps the functional requirements of the "TOM GEAR" project to physical systems, equipment, resources, and organizational structures.

## 1. System & Equipment Allocation
The physical processing and generation capabilities are allocated to specific operational sections.

*   **Energy Generation & Distribution (Postponed)**
    *   *Gasification Reactor*: Allocated to convert wood waste (chips) into gas. [Source: TOM GEAR_input.md]
    *   *Power Generators*: Allocated to generate 700 kW/h of electricity from reactor gas. [Source: TOM GEAR_input.md]
    *   *Control Node*: Allocated for the safe distribution and management of generated electricity and heat. [Source: TOM GEAR_input.md]
    *   *GAP*: Since the gasification unit is officially postponed, the alternative physical source of electricity (e.g., grid connection, diesel generators) is not defined.
*   **Section 1: Sawmill Complex**
    *   *Primary Milling Equipment*: Allocated to process 1500 m³ of roundwood per month over 22-24 working shifts. [Source: TOM GEAR_input.md]
*   **Section 2: Drying & Treatment**
    *   *Press-Vacuum Drying Chambers & Impregnation Units*: Allocated to process up to 1000 m³ of wood per month (operating 30 calendar days). [Source: TOM GEAR_input.md]
*   **Section 3: Clean Moldings**
    *   *Planing & Molding Machinery*: Allocated to produce up to 500 m³ per month of finished goods (lining, decking, floorboards) over 22-24 working shifts. [Source: TOM GEAR_input.md]
*   **Additional Processing (Optional)**
    *   *Small Mechanization Equipment*: Allocated for secondary products like simple pallets and glued moldings. [Source: TOM GEAR_input.md]

## 2. Resource Allocation
*   **Raw Materials (Roundwood)**: 
    *   Target input is 1500 m³ per month. 
    *   *Current State*: Allocation is severely disrupted due to market transformation, high costs, and difficulty acquiring quality raw materials. [Source: TOM GEAR_input.md]
*   **Energy**: 
    *   Target requirement: 700 kW/h. 
    *   *GAP*: Current energy allocation strategy is missing due to the abandonment of the internal gasification plan.
*   **Waste (Wood Chips)**: 
    *   Originally allocated as fuel for the gasification reactor. 
    *   *GAP*: Current waste management/allocation strategy is undefined.

## 3. Human Resources & Organizational Allocation
*   **Production Labor**: 
    *   Required to cover 22-24 shifts/month for milling/moldings and continuous 30-day operations for drying.
    *   *Current State*: Severe deficit in labor resources and required qualifications. [Source: TOM GEAR_input.md]
    *   *GAP*: Exact headcount, shift structures, and specific operational roles are not defined.
*   **Management & Governance**: 
    *   Allocated to a project team / board of directors.
    *   *Current State*: Governance allocation is failing due to internal competition for final decision-making, broken communication, and loss of trust. [Source: TOM GEAR_input.md]

## 4. Spatial & Facility Allocation
*   **Site Location**: 
    *   A site was selected during the planned realization stages ("Вибір площадки для розміщення"). [Source: TOM GEAR_input.md]
    *   *GAP*: The physical location, total footprint, and spatial layout of the three distinct sections and the energy node are not specified.
