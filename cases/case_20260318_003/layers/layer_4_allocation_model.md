# Layer 4: Allocation Model

This layer maps the functional requirements and processes of the "TOM GEAR" project to physical facilities, equipment, human resources, and external dependencies based on the provided normalized case.

## 1. Physical Architecture & Facilities
The production complex is allocated into distinct physical zones/sections based on the processing stages:

*   **Zone 1: Sawmill Section**
    *   *Allocation*: Primary wood processing.
    *   *Capacity*: 1500 m³ of round timber per month.
    *   *Operating Schedule*: 22-24 working shifts per month.
*   **Zone 2: Drying and Treatment Section**
    *   *Allocation*: Press-vacuum drying, thermal wood processing, and wood impregnation.
    *   *Capacity*: Up to 1000 m³ per month.
    *   *Operating Schedule*: 30 calendar days (continuous operation).
*   **Zone 3: Clean Moldings Section**
    *   *Allocation*: Production of finished goods (lining, clean blanks, decking, floorboards, etc.).
    *   *Capacity*: Up to 500 m³ per month.
    *   *Operating Schedule*: 22-24 working shifts per month.
*   **Zone 4: Energy Generation & Distribution (Currently Postponed)**
    *   *Allocation*: Gas generation reactor and electric generators.
    *   *Status*: Implementation halted ("Відмова від реалізації газогенерації. Перенос на пізніше").

## 2. Equipment & Hardware Allocation
*   **Wood Processing Equipment**: Sawmill machinery, press-vacuum drying chambers, impregnation units, and molding machines.
*   **Small Mechanization Equipment**: Tools for producing additional options like simple pallets and glued moldings ("шляхом малої механізації процесу").
*   **Energy Control Hardware**: Power generation and safe distribution control unit ("Вузол управління електрогенерації та її безпечного розподілу").
*   **Energy Generation Hardware (Postponed)**: Gas generation reactor and electric generators designed for 700 kW/h output.

*   **Board of Management**: Responsible for strategic decisions.

*   **Implementation Team**: Responsible for project execution.
    *   *Current State*: Suffering from communication breakdowns, fatigue, and loss of trust ("Втрата довіри і віри в команду реалізації").
